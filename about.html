Aurora Delouvroy (°1995 , Belgium) studied at the Kunsthumaniora Sint-Lucas Ghent. She completed her studies in Visual Arts. Aurora was admitted into LUCA school of arts and is now obtaining her bachelor’s degree in painting.  Here her interest in contemporary art was stimulated and she started to explore the meaning of 'contemporary'. She uses her own photographic material and transforms them into new images. Aurora only paints details that catch her attention.
She lives and works in Ghent.

Recently Aurora was inspired by religious images. She is attempting to integrate the old into the new. The angle of the viewer is hereby important. Also the innocence and desirability that woman are expected to have. The viewer sees the subject from below which expresses humility. The judgement that people have over each other is a phenomenon that facinates her. Thats why she displayes herself, she wants to portay herself as most people see her.
